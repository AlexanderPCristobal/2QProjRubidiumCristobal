Endings

Undertale has three main endings, namely: Neutral, True Pacifist, and Genocide routes. Each route has different causes and effects depending on what the player does. There is a fourth miscellanous ending, Hard Mode, if the player seeks to have a more difficult challenge.

  • Neutral
  • True Pacifist
  • Genocide
  • Hard Mode

Neutral Route

This ending is the most common ending achieved by players who do not know anything about the game going into it. It is achieved by meeting one of the following conditions:

  • The player does not befriend all pivotal characters (Papyrus, Undyne, and Alphys).
  • The True Lab has not been explored
  • The player kills any, but not every, monster

This route ends with the player going to the surface alone. There are multiple variations of Sans judging the player at the end, depending on the choices made throughout the playthrough.

True Pacifist Route

This ending can be achieved by meeting all of the conditions below:

  • A Neutral Route has been completed.
  • No monsters were killed throughout the playthrough.
  • Papyrus, Undyne, and Alphys were all befriended.
  • The True Lab has been explored.

This route ends with the barrier being broken, letting all monsters escape the Underground.

Genocide Route

This ending is achieved by killing all monsters, including the bosses, in each area until no one remains.

This route ends with the destruction of the game's world. When the game is relaunched after a completed Genoicde Route, Chara asks the player to give up their soul in exchange for the recreation of the world. If the player says yes, the game erases all data, but it permanently changes the endings of any later True Pacifist or Genocide routes. These endings are popularly called Soulless Pacifist and Soulles Genocide. Neutral endings are unaffected.

Hard Mode

This mode is activated when the player names themselves "Frisk" at the start of the game. This increases the difficulty of all monster encounters, as well as change some dialogue in the game. This mode only lasts until the end of the Ruins. After defeating Toriel, the Annoying Dog comes in to announce the end of Hard Mode.
